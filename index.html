ABOUT US

______________________________

Audiocastle was founded in 2011 and has since produced music for hundreds of media projects in advertising and film. Some of the top brands of the world have trusted Audiocastle to produce music for their campaigns including Corona, Microsoft, DHL, Reebok, Natures Way, Ford, The Special Olympics, American Express, Patron and many more.

Audiocastle is also known for their award-winning film scores which have appeared in theaters, television, streaming, and in hundreds of festivals all over the world. Feature films they have scored including “Uncle John” and “Late Life” can be currently seen on Netflix. Their latest release, one of 6 movies to be released this year, “Omniboat” premiered at the Sundance Film Festival in 2020.
